Type in a 3-Letter or 4-Letter String and get a Set of Options of HexValues that Decode to that String.
Direct encoding works best with 3 Letter Strings and Base62 encoding works best for 4 Letter Strings. But you can do Whatever.
Seriously though. I recommend a 4-Letter Word on Base62 Mode. If you only have a 3-Letter Word, add a 0 at the end or something.
Note: If the hex has 6 characters, it's RGB; 8 characters is RGBA. I think 4 character hex colors mean something but I forget.
